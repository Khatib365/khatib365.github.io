I'm Khatib — Technical Lead for Power Platform and CRM at Trax Group, Saudi Arabia. I run both departments, overseeing solution architecture, ALM governance, team delivery, and client work across Dynamics 365, Dataverse, Azure, and the full Power Platform stack.
